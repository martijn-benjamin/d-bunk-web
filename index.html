What is Filtr?

Filtr is an open source initiative aimed at providing better insights on the credibility of online publishers.

Public rating will be used to provide visuals on pages you visit reflecting the public opinion of that publisher.

With this we aim to have hoax and clickbait type of publications lose their intended effect.

Supported Browsers

 

Chrome

Firefox

Safari

Currently we are working on the Chrome extension. With the extension users can rate the publishers they visit and based on the collected data the extension will provide visual information in the browser reflecting the public opinion on that publisher. We plan to offer the same functionality for Firefox and Safari.
