What is Filtr?

Filtr is an open source initiative aimed at providing better insights on the credibility of online publishers.

Public rating will be used to provide visuals on pages you visit reflecting the public opinion of that source.

With this we aim to have hoax and clickbait type of publications lose their intended effect.

Supported Browsers

 

Chrome

Firefox

Safari

Currently we are working on the Chrome extension. With the extension users can rate the publications they visit and based on the collected data the extension will provide visual information in the browser reflecting the public opinion of that publication. We plan to offer the same functionality for Firefox and Safari.

Collaborate

Keep the debate alive

Being an open source project on a very debatable subject, we want to involve as many people as we can and take the time to have an open discussion about it.
